Everything you need.

No bloat, no subscriptions. Just remote access that works.

🌐

Browser-based

No client install needed. Just open a URL in any modern browser and connect to your device.

⚡

Low latency

Built on WebRTC for peer-to-peer connections. Traffic goes directly between devices when possible.

🔒

Self-hosted

Run the relay server on your own hardware. Your data never touches third-party servers.

📁

File transfer

Drag and drop files between your local and remote machine. Fast transfers over the P2P connection.

📋

Clipboard sync

Copy on one device, paste on the other. Text and images sync automatically.

🛜

NAT traversal

Works behind firewalls and school networks. STUN/TURN handles the hard parts for you.
